Background
  • In some clinical trials, we may have to collect data about physiological and morphological findings related to cardiovascular system, including the heart, blood vessels and lymphatic vessels.
  • Some example data points are:
    • Blood flow rate
    • Cardiac output
    • Left Ventricular Ejection Fraction
    • Peak Blood Flow Velocity
  • SDTM standard has a standard domain for capturing these findings - "Cardiovascular System Findings (CV)"
  • CV dataset belongs to Findings class and has an expected record structure of 'One record per finding or result per time point per visit per subject'

What type of information is typically collected or presented?
  • Date of assessment
  • Test performed status
    • Yes
    • No 
      • Reason not performed
  • Short name of the test
  • Name of the test
  • Result
    • in original units
    • in standard units
  • Derived information
    • Baseline flag - when results of a test are collected over pre and post treatment period
