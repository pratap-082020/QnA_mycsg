Background
  • In some clinical trials, we may have to collect data about diagnosis of the cause of death for a subject
  • Some example data points are:
    • Autopsy Indicator
    • Hospitalized at Time of Death Indicator
    • Information Source for Cause of Death
    • Location of Death
    • Primary Cause of Death
    • Secondary Cause of Death
  • SDTM standard has a standard domain for capturing these findings - "Death Details (DD)"
  • DD dataset belongs to Findings class and has an expected record structure of 'One record per finding per subject'

 

What type of information is typically collected or presented?
  • Date of assessment
  • Short name of the test
  • Name of the test
  • Result/finding
    • in original units
    • in standard units
  • Evaluator of the response/finding
