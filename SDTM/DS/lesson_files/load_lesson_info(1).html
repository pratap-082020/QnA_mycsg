Background

The typical flow of a participant in clinical trial is to 

  • provide consent to participate in the study - informed consent
  • undergo screening procedures
  • be assigned to one of the treatment groups of the study - randomization
  • get treated for the specified period - end of treatment
  • followed up for certain period post treatment completion
  • end the study - end of study
  • Data is collected in case report forms to account a subject for all the above steps
  • Data related to informed consent, randomization to study treatment is typically called as 'protocol milestones'
  • Data related to the completion/discontinuation status of treatment or study is called as 'disposition event'
  • Disposition (DS) dataset is used to provide an accounting for all subjects who entered the study in terms of protocol milestones and disposition events.
