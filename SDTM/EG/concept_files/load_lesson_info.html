Background
  • In some clinical trials, we may have to collect data about different parameters from electrocardiogram (ECG) and its interpretation
  • Some example data points are:
    • PR interval
    • ST interval
    • QT interval
    • QRS duration
    • RR interval
    • Interpretation
  • SDTM standard has a standard domain for capturing these findings - "ECG Test Results (EG)"
  • EG dataset belongs to Findings class and has an expected record structure of 'One record per finding or result per time point per visit per subject'

What type of information is typically collected or presented?
  • Date of assessment
  • Test performed status
    • Yes
    • No 
      • Reason not performed
  • Short name of the test
  • Name of the test
  • Position of the subject when test was performed
  • Method of test or examination
  • Result
    • in original units
    • in standard units
  • Derived information
    • Baseline flag - when results of a test are collected over pre and post treatment period
