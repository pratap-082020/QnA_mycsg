Background
  • In every clinical trial, each patient will be enrolled into the study only if they meet prespecified conditions mentioned in the study protocol
  • These 'prespecified conditions' are called 'Inclusion criteria' and 'Exclusion criteria'
  • Subject should meet all inclusion criteria to be eligible for enrollment
  • Subject should NOT meet any exclusion criteria 
  • SDTM standard has a standard domain for capturing these findings - "Inclusion/Exclusion Criteria Not Met (IE)"
  • Note that we only capture the information for the inclusion criteria not met and exclusion criteria met in IE dataset
  • IE dataset belongs to Findings class and has an expected record structure of 'One record per inclusion/exclusion criterion not met per subject'

What type of information is typically collected or presented?
  • Date of assessment
  • Short name of the test
  • Name of the test
  • Result
    • in original units
    • in standard units (converted to standard terminology of Y/N)

Other notes
  • In most studies, IE criteria will be evaluated only once at the time of screening
  • In some studies, IE criteria will be evaluated more than once, for example at screening visit for the first time and subsequently at the visit of randomization for the second time.
  • In the above cases, we will present both instances in IE - using VISITNUM/VISIT to differentiate between these two instances of IE criteria evaluation
