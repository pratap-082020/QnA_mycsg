Background
  • In clinical trials, the participants will undergo specified examinations at predetermined timepoints called visits
  • Each visit will typically be planned on a specific day (eg: 7 days from start of the treatment, 14 days from start of treatment etc)
  • This pre-specified day for a visit is generally called the target day for the visit
  • Sometimes, protocols allow the visits to happen plus or minus a few days from the target day
  • So, for a specific visit, there can be different dates across datasets
  • We use SV (subject visits) domain to capture the start and end dates of each of the visit
  • Sometimes, subjects will have visits other than the planned visit and we call these as 'unplanned visits'.
  • In this lesson, we will see how to create SV domain for a hypothetical study
